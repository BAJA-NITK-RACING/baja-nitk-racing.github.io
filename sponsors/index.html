OUR SPONSORS

Our team benefits from an amazing set of sponsors who support us to dream, design, build and race!

WHY SPONSOR US?

ONLINE & MEDIA EXPOSURE

Team Baja NITK Racing has active social media handles in Instagram, Facebook and LinkedIn that has thousands of right followers through which we publicize sponsors via shoutouts and digital content generated explicitly for them.

INCIDENT EXPOSURE

Team BNR has a dedicated marketing and publicity team, that works to publicize our sponsors during events of NITK's Annual Cultural Fest, Incident,that reach out to 40,000+ students from 200+ colleges.

ENGINEER EXPOSURE

Baja NITK Racing hosts dedicated stalls during Engineer, NITK's Annual Technical fest & South India's 2nd largest Tech Fest, with a footfall of 25000+ students from 150+ colleges. The sponsors are publicized to a crowd of enthusiastic engineering students and industry experts that visit the campus from all over India.

NATIONAL LEVEL COVERAGE

The BAJA SAE India competition is telecast nationally, and globally on youtube. Our sponsors will receive national level branding in the form of stickers on our car, tshirts, banners in our stalls.
